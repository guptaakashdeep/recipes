Ingredients:

1 tbsp instant coffee

¼ cup warm water, 62.5 ml

3 to 4 tbsp sugar or add as required

2 cups chilled milk, 500 ml

6 to 8 ice cubes or as required.

INSTRUCTIONS
in a blender take take 1 tbsp instant coffee, 3 to 4 tbsp sugar, add ¼ cup warm water.
add ¼ cup warm water. stir very well. to make the shake, i used full cream milk. you can also use skimmed milk or low fat milk. if using skimmed milk or low fat milk, then just reduce the ¼ cup water to about 2 to 3 tbsp.
blend for a minute or till the coffee solution becomes frothy and the color lightens a bit.
add 6 to 7 ice cubes. for a thick cold coffee, you can reduce the ice cubes from 2 to 4.
pour 2 cups chilled milk (150 ml).
blend once more till everything is mixed well and you get a nice froth on top.
pour in glasses and serve cold coffee. the froth on top settles down and reduces with time. so serve coffee milk shake immediately.
